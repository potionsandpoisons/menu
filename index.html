Elixirs

Fermenting various ingredients in alcohol to create a liqueur has been a practice in many different cultures around the world for millennia. We use a method passed down and practiced for generations by Koreans to create our elixirs that have a wide array of health benefits. All of our elixirs are high in antioxidants (flavanoids, carotenoids, phenolic acid) that protect cells from free radicals that can cause diseases. They are rich in vitamins, minerals and promote bone health, blood sugar control, and help boost immune system function. Each elixir has further alleviative properties specific to the ingredient.

*Any form of alcohol not taken in moderation may be detrimental to your health.
